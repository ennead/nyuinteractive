Materials Management

Just as the first floor unifies all the public space and circulation across the campus, the lower floor is a comprehensive service level connecting all buildings. Ennead developed major new materials management facilities, including clean and soiled docks. Automated guided vehicles move supplies, medicine, and meals through a new network of service tunnels.
