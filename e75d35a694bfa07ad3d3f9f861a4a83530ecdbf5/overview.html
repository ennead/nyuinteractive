Overview

NYU Langone Manhattan Campuses

NYU Langone Medical Center occupies a ten-acre superblock on the east edge of Manhattan, a site that was consolidated and deeded to NYU by New York City in 1949. The campus developed incrementally over four decades, following a modernist “tower-in-the-park” master plan. More recently, NYU Langone developed a stand-alone orthopedic hospital about a mile to the south.
